Global AI Bootcamp 2025

DeepSeek shook the global AI community, making Hangzhou the first city to become popular by 2025 due to open-source large models. The “Hangzhou Six Little Dragons” has become a demonstration of the vigorous vitality of the city’s scientific and technological innovation.

On March 22nd, Global AI Bootcamp 2025 will come to Hangzhou, where a number of senior technical experts and developers in Hangzhou will focus on the practical application of the latest open source large models, and learn how to use AI to achieve x10 capabilities in their daily work.

Join us to stay up-to-date on the latest AI technologies and explore a smarter AI future.

Main Organizer

Bin Xiang

Microsoft Most Valuable Professional, One of the organizers of FireUG, Organizer at Global AI Bootcamp

Agenda

  • [14:00-14:15] Introduction

    Weiyu Xiao

    Microsoft Most Valuable Professional, One of the organizers of FireUG, NetCorePal Framework Developer

  • [14:15-15:00] Deep Impact of DeepSeek

    Hao Hu

    20-years Microsoft Most Valuable Professional, 18-year Microsoft Certified Trainer, Organizer and Speaker at Global AI Bootcamp / Global Azure Bootcamp / Global M365 Bootcamp / Global Power Platform Bootcamp

    Session Overview:

    DeepSeek has undoubtedly secured its place in the hottest AI discussions recently. But why does the release of this model create such a huge impact? In this session, we will explore its technical features in an easy-to-understand way and discuss how to refine model training and inference to achieve more powerful reasoning chains at a lower cost. What are the key differences between on-premises and cloud deployment? Can models on Azure AI be fine-tuned and distilled? Join us for an engaging discussion!

  • [15:00-15:45] Smart Office Work, DeepSeek Leads the Way

    QingLin Zhou

    23-years Microsoft Most Valuable Professional, ExcelHome.net founder, webmaster, technical author, consultant, and trainer.

    Session Overview:

    In the fast-paced workplace, efficiency is competitiveness. DeepSeek, as a powerful AI office tool, is revolutionizing workplace efficiency. How does it help us break free from tedious tasks? Through practical cases and operational demonstrations, we will delve into various efficient solutions for seamlessly using DeepSeek and share 7 practical workplace tips. How can DeepSeek be integrated with Excel, Word, WPS, or other AI tools to achieve a smarter office experience? Let’s explore together and open a new chapter in workplace efficiency!

  • [14:40-14:50] Q&A
